## 🎲 Random Aspects
Leave some aspects to fate! These player characteristics are randomly generated.

* **Height:** Heights are determined by position.
* **Weight:** Similar to height, weight is influenced by position.
* **Wingspan:** Wingspans are randomly assigned within a broad range (40-100).
* **Jumpshot:** Based on your player's height, a jump shot animation is automatically assigned. Don't worry, you can personalize this later with earned XP!

## 📏 Important Physicals

Let's delve into the physical aspects that define your player.

* **Height:** Taller heights lower the speed, agility, and vertical attributes.
* **Weight:** Weight is purely cosmetic. 
* **Strength:** Strength is randomly generated based on position.

## 💎 Anomalies

### Benefits

* **Growth Spurt:** A random height boost between 1 to 3 inches.
* **Extra Cash:**  Anomalies start with a 30% increase in starting currency.
* **Physical Boost:** Physical attributes will be increased randomly by 5 to 10 points.

> Each player has a **1.5%** chance to transform into an anomaly upon creation.

## 🚫 Height Limits

- **1️⃣ PG:** 6'5
- **2️⃣ SG:** 6'7
- **3️⃣ SF:** 6'9
- **4️⃣ PF:** 6'11

> These are not the maximum heights for player creations. Instead, they provide a guideline for out-of-position players who are trying to play a different position.

## 🟩 Mandatory Surge Year

All new players will be required to play a mandatory year in the Surge before joining a team. 

> This policy ensures that these players develop their skills and are fully prepared for the competitive intensity of our league.

## Teams & Contracts

**Salary Cap**

Our league operates with a clear salary cap of $1500.

> Teams who are at $1500 or below during the season can take on upto $1600 in contracts through trades during the season.

**Contract Tiers**

* **$150–$300:** All players are eligible.
* **$300–$350:** Must have played one season.
* **$350–$400:** Must have played two seasons.
* **$400–$500:** Must have played two seasons and been awarded with an All-Star or All-TRC 2nd award.
* **$500–$600:** Must have played two seasons and been awarded a MVP, DPOY, or All-TRC 1st award.

**Contract Clauses**

* **No Trade Clause:** Exclusive to supermax contracts, restricting trades without player consent.
* **No Cut Clause:**  No limitations; any player can be offered a contract with this clause.

**Contract Options**

These clauses can be negotiated during new contracts or extensions.

**Contract Splits**

Contracts are divided between SP and XP.

| Player | Amount | SP (30%) | XP (70%) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Player 1 | $600 | 180SP | 420XP |

## **Trades**

### **Trade Approval**
- All trades must receive **administrator approval**.
- Approval is based on the rationale behind the trade, not just its balance.
> Teams under new management are not shielded from making poor trade decisions.

### **Trade Restrictions**
- **Player Trades:** A player must play a minimum of **three games** with their current team before being eligible for trade.
- **Draft Picks:** Tradeable only if the pick is not more than **two years in advance**.

### **Trading Over The Cap**
- Teams can trade up to **$1,600** of their total salary cap during the season.
- **Important:** If your team is over the **$1,500 salary cap**, you cannot execute a trade unless it brings your cap under $1,500.

---

## **Contracts**

### **Contract Clauses**

#### **No-Trade Clause (NTC)**
- Must be exercised within **24 hours** of the trade posting in the pending trades channel.

#### **No-Cut Clause (NCC)**
- A player with an NCC must request their own release with team approval.
- For inactive players, a release requires **administrator approval**.

### **Releasing Contracts**
- **Active Players:** Require both the player’s agreement and administrator approval. Note that active players making above the minimum cannot be released in the first season.
- **Inactive Players:** Can be released with administrator approval.
- **NCC Players:** Release necessitates approval from both the player and the administrator.

### **Signing Surge Players**
- Surge players are available on a **first-come-first-serve** basis (excluding rookies and stashes).
- They sign for a minimum one-year deal at **$150**.
- Each surge team may veto one call-up request per season. Additionally, if a veto is exercised, that team is barred from making another call-up request for the same player during that season.

### **Signing Free Agents**

#### **In-Season Free Agency**
- Teams may **claim** players at the minimum contract amount ($150) following the waiver order.
- Note: Rookies are ineligible as they must complete a mandatory season in the Surge league.

#### **Restructuring Contracts**
- Each team can restructure one contract during the off-season. 
- **Restructure Definition:** This is considered a completely new contract agreement.

---

## **Blow It Up!**

Once every five seasons, a team can opt to use the **Blow It Up!** feature, which clears the entire roster and places all players on the free agent market. Eligibility requires:

- A **losing record** in the most recent season.
- **Missing the playoffs** in the last two consecutive seasons.

# **Manager Points System**

![A basketball player animation.](https://i.imgur.com/IXGrvVh.png)

Manager Points (MP) serve as a **performance evaluation system** for team managers. By making strategic moves, achieving success on the court, and maintaining strong decision-making, managers accumulate points. At the end of each season, their status is re-evaluated based on these points, determining whether they retain their position.

---

## **How Manager Points Work**

### **Team Performance Metrics**
Success on the court directly affects a manager's MP. Each game and postseason achievement contributes to a manager’s overall score.

| Event | Impact on MP |
|-------|------------|
| **Win (W)** | +4 points per win |
| **Loss (L)** | -2 points per loss |
| **Playoff Appearance (PA)** | +10 points |
| **Finals Appearance (FA)** | +10 points |
| **Championship Win (CW)** | +25 points |
| **Losing Season (LS)** *(negative win-loss record)* | -10 points |
| **Swept in Playoffs (SP)** *(lost series 0-3)* | -5 points |
| **Missed Playoffs (MPL)** | -10 points |

---

## **Evaluation & Job Security**
At the end of each season, a manager’s total MP is assessed.

- **Managers falling at or below 10 MP will be dismissed** by their team’s ownership.
- If a manager has a losing season but maintains 50+ MP, their points will be reset to 50, then evaluated.
- Each championship win adds +25 MP to the 50 MP reset value, allowing long-term success to impact future evaluations. For example:
    - 1 championship → reset to 75 MP
    - 2 championships → reset to 100 MP
    - 3 championships → reset to 125 MP

---

## **Ownership Influence on Manager Points**
Each team has an **owner** with unique priorities. Meeting or failing to meet these priorities affects MP by **2-10 points**, depending on the weight of the category.

[View Current Ownership Priorities](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1bDxHNuVN7tf-28hLA2cfH5f1iUZHCJ6-kFC60HPsrYM/edit?usp=sharing)

### **Revenue Star System**
Revenue is a key factor in managerial success. Each team’s revenue rating (1 to 5 stars) **modifies MP impact** as follows:

| Revenue Rating | MP Impact |
|---------------|----------|
| **1 Star**  | ±2 points |
| **2 Stars** | ±4 points |
| **3 Stars** | ±6 points (double impact) |
| **4 Stars** | ±8 points |
| **5 Stars** | ±10 points (maximum impact) |

For example, a **manager with 3 stars in revenue** will gain or lose **6 MP** based on their revenue performance. A **5-star manager** could see a swing of **10 MP**, making financial success a crucial aspect of team management.

> Revenue is just one of the many star systems. However, they all follow this same system.

---

## **Why This System Matters**
- **Data-Driven Accountability:** Provides a structured way to assess managerial success.
- **Encourages Competitive Teams:** Rewards good team-building and strategic planning.
- **Promotes Active Engagement:** Keeps managers invested in both on-court and off-court decisions.
- **Balanced Job Security:** Gives managers room to recover from setbacks while rewarding long-term success.

By integrating performance, decision-making, and financial success, this system ensures that **only the best managers stay at the helm** of their teams!
